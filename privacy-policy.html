Privacy policy

FX Daily is designed to be simple and privacy‑friendly. We do not ask you to create an account and we do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or payment details.

Local data

Some features, such as favorites, recent conversions, and reminder preferences, are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This data stays on your device and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser data.

Third‑party services

FX Daily uses a third‑party API to retrieve exchange rates. Your IP address may be visible to that provider as part of normal web requests.

If advertising is enabled, ads may be served by third‑party networks such as Google AdSense. These services may use cookies or similar technologies to measure performance and prevent fraud. You can manage your preferences through your browser settings and any controls provided by the ad network.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact the site owner using the contact details provided on the hosting platform or domain registration.
