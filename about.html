About FX Daily

FX Daily is a lightweight, installable web app designed for people who check exchange rates often and want a clean, distraction‑free experience.

You can convert between major currencies in seconds, save your favorite pairs, and quickly revisit your recent conversions. The app is optimized for mobile browsers and can be installed to your home screen like a native app.

Where do rates come from?

Exchange rates are provided by a third‑party foreign exchange API. Rates are updated frequently, but they may differ slightly from the prices offered by your bank, card issuer, or money transfer service.

Who is this for?

FX Daily is built for travelers, online shoppers, freelancers, and anyone who needs a quick sense of what their money is worth in another currency.

FX Daily does not provide financial advice. Always confirm important amounts with your bank or provider.
