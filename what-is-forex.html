What is Forex?

The foreign exchange market (often called “forex” or “FX”) is where currencies are traded. It is the largest financial market in the world, with trillions of dollars exchanged every day.

People trade currencies for travel, business, investing, and international payments. Exchange rates constantly change based on supply, demand, interest rates, and global events.

FX Daily helps you check these rates quickly and clearly, without the noise or complexity found on traditional financial platforms.

Why does forex matter?

Whether you're traveling abroad, shopping online, freelancing internationally, or sending money to family, exchange rates affect how much you actually pay or receive.
